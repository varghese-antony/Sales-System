## What the system actually does (Tech Head's summary for your context)

1. **Auto lead generation** — pulls fresh prospects from Google Maps every morning,
   scores them, finds verified emails, deduplicates. Runs without anyone touching it.

2. **Email sequences** — 3-touch automated sequences, scheduled sends, open tracking,
   auto follow-ups, replied detection.

3. **AI research + personalised outreach** — click any lead → Research → AI reads their
   website, identifies pain points, writes a personalised email AND LinkedIn DM in ~15 seconds.

4. **LinkedIn connection tracker** — logs sent requests, flags who accepted, queues the DM.

5. **Demo Mode** — one toggle blurs all names/emails on screen (used in this recording).

**Stack:** Next.js + JavaScript, GitHub, Supabase, runs locally.
